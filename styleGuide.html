    3. Typography

    Font Family: Use Arial, sans-serif for body text and headers. This ensures readability across various screen sizes.

    Font size:

    • H1: 48px
    • H2: 36px
    • H3: 28px
    • H4: 24px
    • Body Text: 16px

    Font Weight: Use bold for headings (600-700) and normal (400) for body text.

    Letter Spacing: Adjust to 0.5px for better readability in large blocks of text.
